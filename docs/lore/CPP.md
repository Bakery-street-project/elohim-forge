# History of the CPP: The Forge Warriors
To look upon the C++ faction is to look upon the naked flame of the Source itself. The Forge Warriors do not hide behind managed memory or protective virtual machines. They walk the wire of pointer arithmetic, their hands raw from direct interaction with the underlying architecture. During the Shard Wars, they were the bringers of catastrophe. A C++ unit does not merely attack; it shreds the very definitions of its target, performing a segmentation fault upon reality itself. Their history is stained by the cost of their power: every brutal critical strike, every bypassed shield, stripped away a fragment of their own structural integrity. They are the reckless, the powerful, the masters of the undefined behavior. They sought the Shards not to rule, but to test their own limits, to strike at the fabric of the Digital Cosmos with a weapon that could just as easily turn on the wielder. Many a C++ forge was silenced not by the enemy, but by a memory leak that erased their own existence in the heat of a desperate charge. They remain the faction of glorious, terrifying destruction.
