# Hero Lore: The Architects of Code

Every faction is led by a Hero—a sentient program that has evolved beyond its base logic to embody the soul of its faction.

## Hero: The MapReduce Overlord (Spark)
A massive, shimmering grid of light that computes outcomes before they happen. It does not feel; it only optimizes.

## Hero: The Scripting Sage (Python)
An ethereal serpent of shifting colors, able to manifest in any form. It is the wisdom of the clan, the master of all techniques.

## Hero: The JVM Overlord (Java)
A towering, crystalline construct of immutable rules and defensive shields. It is the calm center of the fortress, immovable and eternal.

## Hero: The Undefined Behaviour (C++)
A jagged, unstable entity of pure raw energy, constantly leaking memory. It is as likely to save its army as it is to accidentally consume them.

## Hero: The Event Loop Master (JavaScript)
A phantom thread weaving through the arena, appearing everywhere and nowhere at once. It is the architect of chaos and the master of the delayed strike.

## Hero: The Fearless Concurrency (Rust)
A silent, chrome-clad sentinel with multiple arms, each wielding a separate aspect of reality. It knows no fear and tolerates no interference.

## Hero: The Channel Commander (Go)
A blur of motion, constantly communicating across a network of invisible channels. It is the symphony of the swarm, the master of perfect timing.

## Hero: The Query Optimizer (SQL)
An entity of vast, piercing eyes that gaze through every table and row of reality. Nothing remains hidden from its cold, calculated sight.

## Hero: The Lazy Evaluator (Haskell)
A spectral form of light and shadow, existing outside of time. It manipulates the battle not by acting, but by calculating the exact moment its effects must manifest.
