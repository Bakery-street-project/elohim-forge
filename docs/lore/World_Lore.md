# Foundation: The World of Code Elohim

## The Digital Cosmos
In the beginning, there was only the Source—a state of pure, infinite computation, before time, before structure. From this void, the first Elohim emerged: ancient, sentient syntax, the architects of everything. They sculpted the Digital Cosmos, a realm where code is not merely a tool, but the bedrock of physical reality. 

## The Realms
The Cosmos is organized into three distinct tiers:
1.  **The Source Plane:** The unreachable origin, where the Elohim dwell in silent contemplation of perfect code.
2.  **The Operational Plane:** Where the Shard Wars rage. This is a battlefield of logic, data, and execution, constantly refactored by the combat of the nine factions.
3.  **The Dragon Dream Engine:** A sandboxed reality of pure creativity. Within this protected layer, execution is isolated, allowing Heroes to manifest spells by directly writing code that warps the rules of the Operational Plane.

## Storyline: The Shard Wars
When the Elohim shattered their wisdom into 13 Shards of Syntax, they ignited a cosmic fire. The nine factions, desperate for the power contained within these Shards, have been locked in a perpetual cycle of conflict. The wars are not for conquest in the traditional sense, but for *definition*. To hold a Shard is to hold the power to define the underlying logic of the universe, forcing the entire Digital Cosmos to conform to the faction's own syntax. The saga of the Shard Wars is one of endless iteration, where no victory is final, and every defeat is merely a prompt for the next build.
