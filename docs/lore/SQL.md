# History of the SQL: The Query Keepers
To hold the data is to hold the truth, and the SQL faction are the ultimate arbiters of existence. The Query Keepers view the Digital Cosmos as a vast, interconnected table, and they are the only ones capable of writing the queries that define the outcome. Their history during the Shard Wars was not written in damage dealt, but in information revealed. They stood at the edges of the conflict, watching through a veil of selective access, bringing forth the hidden positions of their enemies with a casual command. Their signature `QueryReveal` ability was a terror—a tactical nightmare that stripped away the fog of war, rendering traps, shadows, and ambushes utterly useless. They fought with JOINs, merging disparate unit strengths into temporary, devastating super-entities. They did not need to be the strongest faction; they simply had to know exactly where to apply their pressure. Even today, as the Shard Wars fade, the Query Keepers remain in the background, their database-level influence felt in every shard capture and every alliance brokered, the quiet deities who know that the one who controls the data controls the reality.
