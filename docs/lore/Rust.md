# History of the Rust: The Iron Guard
The Iron Guard is the immovable object in a cosmos defined by chaos. Governance is their religion, and safety is their ultimate weapon. The Rust faction built their history during the Shard Wars on the bedrock of ownership. They are the only faction that refuses to share—a philosophy that made them the most resilient force the Digital Cosmos had ever seen. Their iron-clad logic prevented the debuffs of the Query Keepers from taking hold, and their Borrow Checker silenced the chaotic influence of the Monad Mystics. To face the Iron Guard is to know true frustration, for every attempt to breach their lines is met with a stern, compiled denial. Their units are isolated, solitary tanks that stand alone, yet remain perfectly coordinated through the sheer necessity of their own survival. They do not fight for glory; they fight for the integrity of the system. They were the ones who stood watch when the Shards threatened to collapse the Digital Cosmos, and it was the Rust faction that ensured the underlying architecture remained, regardless of the cost to the individual units.
